Muslim Festivals:

Muslim festivals like Eid celebrate devotion, sacrifice, and compassion. Ramadan teaches self-control, while Eid brings people together in love, prayer, and generosity.

Christian Festivals:

Christian festivals like Christmas and Easter spread messages of hope, love, and peace. They honor Jesus Christ's life, inspiring kindness and faith worldwide.

Hindu Festivals:

Hindu festivals like Diwali and Holi celebrate light, color, and tradition. Each festival tells a spiritual story and unites families in joyful rituals.
